Recipe #1: Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
  • Difficulty: Easy
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 1 tbsp
Oil 1 tbsp

Step 1: Mix flour, milk, egg, and sugar in a bowl.

Step 2: Heat a pan and add a little oil.

Step 3: Pour some batter and cook both sides until golden.

Recipe #2: Cheese Sandwich

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
  • Difficulty: Very easy
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Cheese 2 slices
Butter 1 tsp

Step 1: Butter the bread.

Step 2: Put cheese between the slices.

Step 3: Fry in a pan until the bread is golden and cheese melts.
